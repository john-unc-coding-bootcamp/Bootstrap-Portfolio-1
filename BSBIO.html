My name is Xavier! I am many things a athlete, a teacher, a coach and soon a web developer! I was born in Morgantown, WV but I grew up in Ellicott City, Md. After I graduated high school I attended North Carolina Central University. While in attendance I joined the football team. Upon graduation I received a BA in History and went on to play pro football. After my "playing days" ended I turned my attention to one of my first loves, education.
